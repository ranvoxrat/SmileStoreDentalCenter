Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins or is an appointment required?

We recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure availability, but we do our best to accommodate walk-ins and dental emergencies.

How can I book an appointment?

You can call us, send a message on our social media pages, or book online through our website.

Is there parking available?

There are parking spaces available in the building, but they are on a first-come, first-served basis.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and selected e-wallets. We also offer payment plans for treatments that require multiple visits such as braces, dentures, and root canals. Just ask your dentist during your consultation.

How much do treatments cost?

Treatment costs vary depending on the procedure. We’ll provide a detailed estimate after your consultation, and we're happy to discuss affordable options.

Will it hurt?

We offer local anesthesia to keep you comfortable during the procedure.
